This is Spain.

Spain, or the Kingdom of Spain is a country located in Southwestern Europe,
with parts of its territory in the Atlantic Ocean, the Mediterranean Sea
and Africa. It was founded on December 6, 1978. It is the largest country in
Southern Europe and the fourth-most populous European Union member state with
a population of 47.42 million. The language spoken in Spain is Castilian Spanish.
